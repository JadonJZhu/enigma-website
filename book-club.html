Book Club

Welcome to Enigma's book club! We read a variety of sci-fi and fantasy books and talk about how cool they are. We meet 2-4 times a quarter. To stay updated with what we are reading and when we are meeting, join us on the Enigma Discord and hit the Books channel!

We also have a book collection located in Powell Library's CLICC Lab that consists of science-fiction and fantasy books donated by club members.
