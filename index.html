Enigma includes everyone from undergrads to graduate students and alumni, encompassing every department from both South Campus and North Campus. If you're interested in science fiction, fantasy, board games, tabletop game, or anything nerdy (even just a bit), you're always welcome with us.

All Enigma meetings are open to the public and are free of charge. There is no formal membership, so drop by any time! Students, staff, faculty, and local community members are all welcome! Seriously, come hang out with us.

Regular meetings will be held every Tuesday at 8:00 PM.
